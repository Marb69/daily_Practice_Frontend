About Us

New Casay Integrated School is a dynamic public educational institution located in the heart of Barangay New Casay. We are committed to providing accessible, quality, and values-oriented education from Kindergarten to Senior High School. As an integrated school, we serve as a vital foundation for young learners, guiding them through their academic journey and shaping their potential as future contributors to society.
